# Hello! I'm Bayasgalan 👋

Hey there! Welcome to my little corner of the internet. My name is **Bayasgalan** — though my
full name is Erdenebat Bayasgalan, Erdenebat is actually my father's name, as is tradition in
Mongolia. So just call me Bayasgalan!

I'm originally from **Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia** 🇲🇳 — a city full of energy, history, and some
seriously cold winters. Since March 2023, I've been living and studying in **South Korea** 🇰🇷
at **Hanyang University ERICA**, where I'm now a 4th year Computer Science student.

---

## What I'm Into Right Now

These days, I'm deep into the world of **AI and LLMs** (Large Language Models). It's one of the
most exciting areas in tech right now, and I'm genuinely obsessed — learning how these systems
work, experimenting with them, and figuring out how to build real things with them. Expect a lot
of posts about that!

When I'm not staring at a screen, you'll probably find me out on my bike 🚴. Cycling is my way
of clearing my head and exploring new places — something I've been doing a lot more since moving
to Korea.

---

## Why I Started This Blog

Honestly? I just want a place to **express myself** — to write about what I'm learning, what I'm
building, and what interests me. No fancy agenda, just a student sharing his journey one post at
a time.

If you're into AI, coding, or curious about life as an international student in Korea, I think
you'll feel right at home here.

Thanks for stopping by — let's learn together! 🙌
